Overview

Our certified inspection station handles all Texas state inspections, including safety and emissions. We check brakes, lights, tires, steering, suspension, and more. If your vehicle fails, we explain the issues and offer affordable repairs to get you back on the road quickly.

What's included

  • Convenient location in Dallas
  • No appointment needed for inspections
  • Clear explanation of any failures

How it works

1

Check-In

Drive in or schedule an appointment. We’ll take your vehicle information and begin the inspection.

2

Inspection

Our certified inspector performs a comprehensive check of all required safety and emissions components.

3

Results

You receive a detailed report. If you pass, we issue your sticker. If not, we provide a repair estimate.
