Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car runs rough, our advanced diagnostic tools identify the problem quickly. We repair everything from sensor replacements to major engine work, always with upfront pricing.

What's included

  • State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment
  • Accurate problem identification
  • Warranty on all repairs

How it works

1

Scan

We connect our diagnostic scanner to read error codes and live data from your engine computer.

2

Analysis

Our technician interprets the data and performs additional tests to confirm the root cause.

3

Repair

We provide a detailed estimate and complete the repair using quality parts, then clear the codes and test drive.
