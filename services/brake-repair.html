Common questions

How do I know if my brakes need service?

Squealing, grinding, a soft pedal, or longer stopping distances are signs. We recommend an inspection every 10,000 miles.

Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?

We use high-quality aftermarket parts that meet or exceed OEM standards, backed by a warranty.
