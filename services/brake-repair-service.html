Common questions

How often should I replace my brakes?

Most pads last 30,000-70,000 miles. We recommend an inspection every 15,000 miles.

Do you offer a warranty on brake work?

Yes, all brake repairs come with a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty. Select parts have a lifetime warranty.
